PARKINSON DISEASE
ABOUT PARKINSON
It is a progressive nervous system disorder that affects movement. It develops gradually, sometimes with a barely noticeable tremor in just one hand. But while tremor may be well-known sign of Parkinson disease, the disorder also commonly causes stiffness or slowing of movement.
POTENTIAL CAUSES
DNA
Environmental Factors
Brain Damage
WHY EARLY DETECTION?
The detection in early stage of Parkinson can greatly assist doctors in providing timely and appropriate treatment to patients. Machine Learning algorithms can analyse patient data and identify patterns that may indicate the presence of Parkinson, enabling healthcare professionals to make informed decisions about diagnosis and treatment.

While there is no known cure for Parkinson disease, there are several lifestyle changes and strategies that may help reduce the risk of developing disease. Some of them are...
